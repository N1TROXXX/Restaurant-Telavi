Traditional Georgian Meat Dish in Tomato Sauce

Experience the heart of Georgian cuisine with our signature meat dish, slow-cooked to perfection in a traditional clay pan. Succulent pieces of tender meat are simmered in a rich, aromatic tomato sauce, infused with a blend of fragrant herbs and spices that embody the flavors of Georgia. The clay pan not only enhances the dish’s presentation but also retains heat, ensuring every bite is warm and comforting.

Adjarian Khachapuri

Indulge in our Adjarian Khachapuri, a beloved Georgian specialty hailing from the Adjara region. This boat-shaped bread features a golden, crispy crust filled with a rich blend of sulguni and imeruli cheeses, topped with a runny egg yolk and a pat of butter. Enjoy it fresh from the oven, perfect for sharing with friends and family. Pair it with a glass of our carefully selected Georgian wine for a delightful dining experience that captures the essence of Georgian culinary tradition!

Kharcho

Kharcho is a beloved traditional Georgian soup, known for its hearty and comforting flavors. It is a staple in Georgian cuisine, especially cherished during the colder months.

Ajapsandali

Ajapsandali is a traditional Georgian vegetable stew that beautifully captures the vibrant flavors of the region. This hearty dish is a celebration of fresh produce, showcasing the best of seasonal vegetables.

Pkhali

Pkhali is a traditional Georgian appetizer that showcases the country’s love for fresh ingredients and bold flavors. This vibrant dish is often made from a variety of finely chopped vegetables, blended with ground walnuts and aromatic herbs.
